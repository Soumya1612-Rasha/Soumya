Soumya Suvra Ghosal

sghosal@umd.edu

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in the CS dept at the University of Maryland, College Park. Before this, I completed my Masters's at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I was advised by Prof. Sharon Li. Previously, I completed my undergraduate studies from Dept of Electronics and Communication Engineering at NIT Durgapur in 2020. I am broadly interested in domain of Robust Deep Learning and Uncertainty Estimation. Specifically, I am interested in solving a critical question: How to make deep-learning models more reliable and robust when deployed in the wild?
